Why we built RankLock

We know how easy it is to get distracted. RankLock is designed to help students notice when they drift away from their study tabs, helping to gently recover a portion of this lost time by building awareness and consistency.

How RankLock Works

Features designed to encourage deep work rather than just tracking the clock.

Active Tab Detection

If you enable Focus Mode, RankLock detects if you minimize the timer or open a new browser tab. It alerts you that you've lost focus, holding you accountable to your session.

Study Progression

Completed study minutes convert into XP. This provides immediate, positive feedback for finishing your daily study blocks, helping you build a consistent habit over time.

Intentional Sessions

You must set a specific topic or task before starting the timer. This simple friction ensures you aren't just starting the clock passively, but have a clear goal in mind.

Session History

Review how many hours you actually spent studying versus how many times you got distracted. Use your own data to find your most productive times of the day.

Consistency Score

Your score drops if you abandon a session midway. It encourages you to sit through the discomfort of a tough physics problem instead of giving up and closing the tab.

Made for Students

No subscription fees, no locked features, and no intrusive advertisements. RankLock is built as a tool, not a monetization platform.

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Ojas Kadam.

I built RankLock because I struggled with consistency during competitive exam preparation. I realized that a standard stopwatch wasn't enough—I needed something that held me accountable when I inevitably tried to open YouTube during a difficult revision block.

RankLock started as a personal project. It doesn't use magic algorithms to make you smarter. It just adds a layer of friction to getting distracted, helping you build better discipline.

Transparency

Privacy & How It Works

Students care about tracking, and you should. Here is exactly what RankLock does and doesn't do:

  • Focus Detection: We use standard browser Visibility APIs to know if the RankLock tab is minimized or hidden.
  • No Browsing History: RankLock cannot see what websites you open or what apps you use.
  • No Data Selling: Your session history stays under your account and is never sold to advertisers.
